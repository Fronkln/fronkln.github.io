Beta Royale

Turkish Page


Beta Royale is a upcoming game that runs on my Source to Unity project
  • A unique take on Battle Royale games
  • Based on the early story of Half-Life 2
  • Every couple of minutes, a toxic gas will fill the map
  • The players will have to collect gasmask canisters to survive the toxic gas but resources are scarce
  • Players will fight eachother with punches, bricks and molotovs to be the last one standing
