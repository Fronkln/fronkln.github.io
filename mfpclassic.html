My Friend Pedro: Classic

Turkish Page


My Friend Pedro: Classic is a mod developed for My Friend Pedro: Blood. Bullets Bananas , the mod's goal is to recreate the original web-game from 2014 in the new 3D game.
  • Official Steam leaderboard support
  • Classic gameplay (no health regeneration, enemies don't drop medkits on death, no checkpoints)
  • Brand new appearances for enemies
  • UI from the web-game
  • MFP Arena will be added
  • The entire story is playable
  • Music from the original game make a return
