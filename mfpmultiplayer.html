My Friend Pedro: Multiplayer

Turkish Page


My Friend Pedro: Multiplayer, is a mod developed for My Friend Pedro: Blood. Bullets Bananas that adds multiplayer support:
  • Totally made with Steamworks API (no absurd third party)
  • Play the story co-op
  • Race against eachother on level time
  • Battle eachother with PvP
  • Customize your character
  • One-click lobbies and efficient networking
